Installation view, The Totem Room, Hal Bromm Gallery, November 19, 1983 – December 23, 1983. Fales, Wojnarowicz's papers, Box 83, slide sheet 75

Hal Bromm Gallery was founded in 1975, the first contemporary art gallery in TriBeCa. For Wojnarowicz's November 1983 show, he recreated a beach by hauling a pile of sand into the gallery, placing his painted totems in the sand.[1]
