Clocktower Gallery

From David Wojnarowicz Knowledge Base
Jump to: navigation, search

The Clocktower is a gallery and studio space associated with P.S. 1 Contemporary Art Center. In 1983, Wojnarowicz gave up his shared studio space on Houston Street. He applied for a new space at the Clocktower through the National Studio Program and was accepted. The following year, he reapplied for another space through the same program. This time he asked for a larger studio that would allow him to work on large-scale motorized constructions and sculptures.[1]
