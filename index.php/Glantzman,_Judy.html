Glantzman, Judy

From David Wojnarowicz Knowledge Base
Jump to: navigation, search

Glantzman is an artist who showed with Civilian Warfare and Gracie Mansion in the 1980s. She and Wojnarowicz became friends in 1985, when both were invited to take part in The Missing Children Show in Louisville, Kentucky. Wojnarowicz hired Glantzman to assist with plans and production of the installation America: Heads of Family/Heads of State at the New Museum in 1990.

Interviewed for Carr, Fire in the Belly, 2012
