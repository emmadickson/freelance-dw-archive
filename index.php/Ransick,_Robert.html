Ransick, Robert

From David Wojnarowicz Knowledge Base
Jump to: navigation, search

Robert Ransick is an artist, designer and educator. From roughly 1991-1993 Ransick was preparator at P.P.O.W. Gallery, where he struck up a friendship with Wojnarowicz.
